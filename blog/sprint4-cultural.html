Having a Growth mindset

What does it mean to have a Growth mindset?
If you have a growth mindset, you believe that it is possible to learn anything you set your mind on to! You understand that what you see, is not what you get and there is potential for growth at any time you wish! If you feel you are bad at math? Someone with a fixed mindset would tell themselves "If I am not good at math now, I will never be" - whereas someone with a growth mindset would rather have the tendency to say "I might not be good at math NOW, but I can be!"

In this exploration, did anything surprise you? Change for you?
I did feel I have been aware of the growth mindset, but without really realising there was more to it than the very basic and being told "you can do anything you set your mind to do". I am now more aware of the differences between having a fixed vs. growth mindset, which have enabled me to reflect more on "which mindset am I in?"
